Email Automation  
SALL Connect integrates external email systems and provides AI‑assisted tools to manage incoming communication.

Email Integration  
Users may connect external email accounts to the platform. This allows incoming messages to be analyzed and organized within the SALL Connect environment.

Automated Drafting  
AI tools can generate draft responses based on message content and user‑defined knowledge databases. Users retain full control over whether to approve or modify these responses.

Workflow Management  
Emails can be scheduled, categorized, and prioritized through AI‑assisted workflows. This helps users manage large volumes of communication more efficiently.
