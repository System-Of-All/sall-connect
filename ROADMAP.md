Development Roadmap  
The development roadmap outlines the planned evolution of SALL Connect across multiple phases. Each phase builds on the previous one, expanding platform capabilities while maintaining stability and alignment with the System Of All ecosystem.

Phase 1 — Platform Foundation  
Core communication and publishing systems form the initial structure of the platform. This phase establishes messaging, voice communication, video meetings, and content publishing tools. The goal is to create a stable environment where users can interact, share information, and collaborate.

Phase 2 — AI Communication Tools  
AI‑driven communication assistance is introduced to enhance user productivity. This includes automated drafting, message analysis, workflow support, and intelligent content suggestions. The focus is on reducing manual workload while ensuring users maintain full control over communication outputs.

Phase 3 — Collaboration Expansion  
Advanced collaboration features are added to support teams, organizations, and structured group work. This phase includes enhanced meeting tools, shared workspaces, coordination systems, and expanded discussion environments. The platform becomes more suitable for professional and organizational use.

Phase 4 — Ecosystem Integration  
SALL Connect integrates with the broader System Of All ecosystem. External services, shared identity systems, cross‑platform communication, and unified governance structures become available. This phase strengthens interoperability and positions SALL Connect as a core component of the larger ecosystem.

Long‑Term Vision  
Future development focuses on modular expansion, deeper AI capabilities, and broader integration with emerging System Of All platforms. The roadmap remains adaptable to evolving user needs, technological advancements, and ecosystem growth.
