Contributing  
Contributions to documentation and platform development are welcome. Contributors are encouraged to follow established development practices and maintain consistency with the standards used throughout the SALL Connect ecosystem. All contributions should reflect the platform’s governance principles, emphasizing responsible participation, clarity, and respect for other contributors.

Contribution Guidelines  
Contributors should ensure that all submissions are accurate, clearly written, and aligned with the platform’s architectural and governance frameworks. Documentation updates should maintain the established formatting style used across the repository. Code contributions should follow standard development workflows, including version control practices, testing procedures, and clear commit history.

Review Process  
All contributions are subject to review before acceptance. Reviews focus on quality, consistency, security considerations, and alignment with platform goals. Feedback may be provided to help contributors refine their submissions. Approved contributions are integrated into the platform according to the development roadmap and release cycles.

Community Expectations  
Participants are expected to engage respectfully, collaborate constructively, and uphold the community standards defined for SALL Connect. Contributors should avoid harmful behavior, respect intellectual property, and ensure that all submitted work adheres to legal and ethical requirements.

Collaboration and Improvement  
The platform evolves through collective effort. Contributors are encouraged to propose improvements, identify issues, and participate in discussions that support the long‑term growth of SALL Connect. Continuous refinement of documentation, features, and governance practices strengthens the ecosystem and supports a reliable, user‑focused platform.
