Platform Architecture  
SALL Connect uses a modular architecture composed of multiple integrated systems.

Core Layers  
Communication Layer  
Messaging, voice communication, and video conferencing.

Content Layer  
Publishing, interaction, and discussion infrastructure.

AI Layer  
Automation, response generation, and content analysis.

Integration Layer  
External email systems and service integrations.

Security Layer  
Monitoring, logging, and safety mechanisms.

Modular Design  
The modular structure allows the platform to evolve through the addition of new systems and integrations without disrupting existing functionality.
