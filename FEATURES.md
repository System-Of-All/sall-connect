Platform Features  
SALL Connect combines multiple systems into a unified digital communication environment.

Social Communication  
Users can publish content, share ideas, and participate in discussions.  
Features include:  
• posts and comments  
• reactions and feedback  
• topic discussions  
• content discovery  
• scheduling and publishing automation  

Messaging System  
Users may communicate directly through messaging channels.  
Capabilities include:  
• direct messaging  
• conversation threads  
• collaborative discussions  
• topic-based communication rooms  

Video Collaboration  
The platform supports real-time collaboration through video meetings.  
Features include:  
• video conferencing  
• meeting recording  
• screen sharing  
• virtual backgrounds  
• participant management  

AI Assistance  
Artificial intelligence assists users in managing communication.  
Capabilities include:  
• automated response suggestions  
• AI-generated message drafts  
• communication workflow automation  
• content categorization  

Email Integration  
External email systems can be connected to the platform.  
Capabilities include:  
• automated email reading  
• response drafting  
• approval workflows  
• automated response scheduling  

Marketing and Promotion  
Users and businesses can promote content and reach audiences through integrated marketing tools.  
Capabilities include:  
• promoted posts  
• marketing campaigns  
• audience targeting tools  
• brand promotion support
