Communication System  
The communication infrastructure of SALL Connect is designed to support both informal interaction and professional collaboration.

Messaging  
Users may communicate directly through private messaging channels. Messaging supports structured conversations and collaborative discussions.

Voice Communication  
The platform supports microphone‑based discussion channels where users can speak directly with other participants. These channels allow spontaneous collaboration around specific topics.

Video Meetings  
Users can create video meetings similar to common collaboration platforms. Capabilities include:  
• scheduled meetings  
• screen sharing  
• meeting recording  
• participant invitations

Discussion Rooms  
Users can open topic‑based discussion rooms to focus conversations on specific subjects. These rooms may serve as temporary collaboration environments.
