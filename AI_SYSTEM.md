AI System  
Artificial intelligence is integrated into SALL Connect to improve communication efficiency and digital coordination. The AI system assists users in drafting messages, organizing information, and automating repetitive communication tasks.

Communication Assistance  
AI systems can generate message drafts based on context and user‑defined knowledge databases. This allows businesses and professionals to maintain consistent communication with clients and partners.

Knowledge Databases  
Users may configure topic‑specific knowledge databases that guide AI behavior. These databases allow AI responses to reflect user‑defined communication styles and informational structures.

Content Analysis  
AI tools analyze platform content to support moderation and safety monitoring. Capabilities include identifying:  
• harmful behavior patterns  
• policy violations  
• potential abuse indicators  
AI systems provide suggestions and indicators but do not replace human oversight.

Automation Workflows  
AI tools may automate repetitive communication tasks such as responding to common questions or organizing messages.
