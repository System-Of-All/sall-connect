Governance  
SALL Connect follows governance principles derived from the System Of All ecosystem. Governance focuses on maintaining responsible platform behavior, protecting users, and ensuring transparency in platform development. Users are responsible for adhering to platform guidelines and respecting other participants. Moderation systems assist in identifying violations but rely on responsible user behavior.

Governance Principles  
The platform is guided by principles that emphasize accountability, fairness, and clarity in decision‑making. These principles ensure that platform rules are applied consistently and that users understand how governance decisions are made.

User Responsibilities  
All participants are expected to engage respectfully, follow platform policies, and contribute to a safe environment. Users must avoid harmful behavior, protect the privacy of others, and report violations when necessary.

Moderation Systems  
Automated systems assist in identifying potential policy violations, harmful behavior, or misuse of platform features. These systems provide indicators and suggestions but do not replace human judgment. Final decisions regarding enforcement actions remain with authorized reviewers.

Transparency  
SALL Connect aims to maintain transparency in how governance decisions are made. Policy updates, enforcement guidelines, and system changes are communicated clearly to ensure users understand how the platform evolves.

Community Standards  
Community standards define acceptable behavior and outline expectations for communication, collaboration, and interaction. These standards support a constructive environment where individuals and organizations can work effectively.
